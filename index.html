HOW TO PLAY

🎮
MOVE
Arrow keys or A/D to move your Bitcoin ship left and right.
🔥
SHOOT
Press SPACE or ↑ to fire at the incoming altcoin army.
📦
POWER-UPS
Crates drop from enemies. Collect them for permanent weapon upgrades.
⚡
SPECIAL UPGRADES
Choose 1 of 3 special abilities every 10 blocks (blocks 1–100) or every 20 blocks (blocks 100–200+). Invincibility, drones, laser and more!
🛸
INSIDER BONUS
Shoot the insider flying across the top for massive bonus points.
🔥
X
Hit enemies in quick succession for 2× or 3× score multipliers.
👑
BOSS FIGHT
Every 50 blocks a powerful crypto exchange boss appears. Defeat it for massive bonus points!
🎁
BOSS REWARDS
Defeat a boss to earn +1 life and choose exclusive ship upgrades.
